Safety Center

Please read and understand the following safety guidelines before using HyperFollow:

1. Role of HyperFollow:

HyperFollow is not a social media service provider. It is merely a platform that connects various third-party follower servers available on the internet in one place. The sole purpose of this app is to help you find these services easily.

2. Your Data is Secure:

HyperFollow does not ask for or collect any of your personal information (username, password, location, etc.). Your privacy is important to us.

3. Login Information and Third-Party Websites:

When you click on each server in the app, you are entering another website. The login information asked there is under the control of those websites. Keep the following in mind when logging into such websites:

  • Do not use original account: Never log in using the password of your main Instagram account.
  • Fake account is mandatory: Create a new (Fake/Dummy) account solely for logging in. After logging in using that account, send the followers to your original ID.
  • Account Security: Providing original account details on such sites may lead to your account being hacked.

4. Liability:

HyperFollow or its developer will not be responsible for any hacking, suspension, or other issues that may happen to your accounts due to the use of third-party websites. Use these services solely at your own risk.
